	Adding Vectors
	\begin{align*}
		\vec{a} = \begin{bmatrix} 6 \\ -2\end{bmatrix}
		\vec{b} = \begin{bmatrix} -4 \\ 4\end{bmatrix}
	\end{align*}

	$\vec{a}, \vec{b} \in \mathbb{R}^2$ \\
	Vectors a, and b are members of $\mathbb{R}^2$

	$\vec{a} + \vec{b} = 
		\begin{bmatrix} 6 + (-4) \\ -2 + 4\end{bmatrix} = 
		\begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}$
